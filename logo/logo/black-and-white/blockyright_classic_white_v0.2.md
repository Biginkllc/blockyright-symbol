## Description

This is the thin-stroke, classic-style Blockyright mark designed for:

- dark backgrounds  
- black documents  
- neon-based graphics  
- UD profile banners  
- high-contrast digital assets  
- night-mode interfaces  

It mirrors the traditional © symbols used in publishing but updates it for the Web3 provenance era.

---

## Recommended Usage

Use this version when:

- the background is black or dark-colored  
- you need a clean, elegant, legal-grade mark  
- the classic ©-style appearance is preferred  
- preparing media for UD, GitHub dark mode, or social branding  
- high readability is required at small sizes
